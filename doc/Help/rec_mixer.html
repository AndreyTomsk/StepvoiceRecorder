Sound Mixer

   The recorder's sound mixer has same lines as the Windows (system) mixer. To open the recording line selection menu just press the button.

   The main recording lines are:

  • Microphone. Recording from a microphone when selected;
  • Line In. Recording from any external sound device like tape recorders;
  • Stereo Mix. Used to record any sounds currently played from your sound card (online radio played in WinAmp or RealPlayer, etc.). Producers name this stream differently, so here are some other possible names: "What U hear", "Output", "Mixer", "Wave", "Sum".
