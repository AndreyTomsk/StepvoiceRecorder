How to register

    If you intend to use StepVoice Recorder after the trial period of 30 days, you must register your copy of StepVoice Recorder.

As a registered user you get the following advantages:

  1. You'll get all future versions for free;
  2. You can use StepVoice Recorder with no limitations;
  3. All the "Shareware Reminder" windows will be removed;
  4. You get extended technical support;
  5. You support further development and improvment of StepVoice Recorder.

    To get the latest licence prices and register your copy on-line using a credit card, please visit the StepVoice Recorder order page located at: http://www.stepvoice.com/order.html

   Most forms of payment are accepted (credit cards, bills, invoice payments, mail, fax, phone orders, etc). Once you have purchased a product by filling in its online order form, a license key will be sent you via email to unlock the software for use.
