System requirements

   The listing below specifies the minimum system requirements for running StepVoice Recorder:

  • Windows98/NT/2000/XP operation system
  • Pentium 300 MHz processor or higher
  • 4 Mb of free memory
  • About 1 Mb of free hard disk space
  • Full-duplex sound card (recommended)
  • Microphone
Note.
   You need a full-duplex (can play and record sounds simultaneously) sound card for recording from the "Stereo Mix" sound stream. It is used to record sounds currently playing on your PC - internet streaming audio, movie sounds, etc.
